# IROK MG63 Dual Engine (双擎) Magnetic Keyboard

The MG63 Dual Engine is a flagship-tier 60% (63-key) magnetic switch keyboard. It is built with a CNC Aluminum alloy chassis and powered by the **StarSpin Dual-MCU** architecture, delivering industry-leading 0.07ms latency and 0.001mm precision for the ultimate competitive edge.

---

## ⚡ Elite Performance Specifications

- **Core Architecture:** **StarSpin Solution (星旋方案)**
- **Processing Power:** **Dual-MCU Full-Chain Direct Link (双MCU全链路直连)**
- **Latency:** **0.07ms (Industry Leading)**
- **Polling Rate:** **8000Hz (True 8K)**
- **RT Accuracy:** **0.001mm (Full-key & Full-range)**
- **Scanning Rate:** 512K Full-key / 147,500K Single-key
- **Switch Type:** **Qinghui Magnetic Switch PRO (清辉磁轴 PRO)**

## 🚀 Why Choose MG63 Dual Engine?

- **StarSpin Dual-MCU Tech:** By using two MCUs, the keyboard achieves a "Direct Link" effect, reducing processing time to a near-instant 0.07ms.
- **Full Aluminum Durability:** The CNC-machined aluminum body provides a heavy, stable base that prevents any keyboard movement during intense gaming.
- **Pro-Grade Accuracy:** The 0.001mm detection accuracy allows players to fine-tune their Rapid Trigger settings to a level of sensitivity that standard mechanical keyboards cannot reach.
- **Compact Efficiency:** The 63-key layout removes unnecessary keys, giving FPS players the maximum possible area for mouse flicking and tracking.
