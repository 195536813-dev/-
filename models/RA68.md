# IROK RA68 Full Aluminum Magnetic Keyboard Series

The RA68 series represents the pinnacle of IROK's design and engineering. It features a premium CNC-machined aluminum chassis, a specialized FR4 gold-bordered plate, and the industry's fastest magnetic switch platforms.

---

## 1. RA68 (StarFlash 480 Edition)
The high-performance variant powered by the **StarFlash 480** platform for extreme precision and 0.08ms response times.

### 🛠 Technical Specifications
- **Performance & Platform:**
  - **Platform/Solution:** **StarFlash 480 (星闪480)**
  - **Latency:** **0.08ms (Esports Grade)**
  - **RT Accuracy:** **0.001mm (Ultra-precision)**
  - **Polling Rate:** 8000Hz (8K)
  - **Scanning Rate:** 256KHz Single-key / 32KHz Full-key
- **Hardware Build:**
  - **Switch:** Qinghui Magnetic Switch (清辉磁轴)
  - **Keycaps:** 
    - Gun Gray: Side-print Shine-through
    - White: Dye-sublimation (热升华)
  - **Ribbon/Tag:** Included (Gun Gray / White matched)
- **Shared Chassis Specs:**
  - **Material:** CNC Full Aluminum Alloy
  - **Layout:** 68% (68 Keys)
  - **Plate:** **FR4 Gold-bordered Positioning Plate**
- **Colors:** Gun Gray / White

## 🚀 Strategic Advantages of the RA68 Series

- **Material Excellence:** The CNC aluminum case combined with an **FR4 Gold-bordered plate** provides a unique typing sound and superior durability that rivals custom mechanical keyboards.
- **StarSpin Architecture (Dual Engine Only):** The dual-MCU setup eliminates processing bottlenecks, providing the most immediate tactile feedback currently possible in esports hardware.
- **0.001mm Precision:** High-fidelity magnetic sensors ensure that Rapid Trigger (RT) settings are consistent across the entire travel of every key.
- **Carbon Crystal Tech:** The Dual Engine version features premium Carbon Crystal keycaps, offering a distinct texture and long-lasting wear resistance.
