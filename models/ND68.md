# IROK ND68 Magnetic Switch Keyboard Series

The ND68 series is a compact 65% (68-key) lineup engineered for high-performance esports. Featuring the 2nd Gen G Turbo algorithm and an esports-grade Gasket mount, it provides extreme responsiveness for competitive gaming.

---

## 1. ND68 (Standard Edition)
A high-speed magnetic keyboard designed for competitive stability and precision.

### 🛠 Technical Specifications
- **Performance & Accuracy:**
  - **RT Accuracy:** **0.005mm**
  - **Polling Rate:** 8000Hz (8K)
  - **Latency:** 0.125ms
  - **Scanning Rate:** 256KHz Single-key / 32KHz Full-key
  - **Platform/Solution:** Rongyuan (容圆)
  - **Algorithm:** 2nd Gen G Turbo (Dynamic Balance & Real-time Temperature Correction)
- **Hardware Build:**
  - **Layout:** 65% (68 Keys)
  - **Structure:** Esports-grade Gasket Mount
  - **Connection:** Wired (High-speed USB-C)
  - **Switch:** Purple Star Magnetic Switch
  - **Plate:** Aluminum Alloy Positioning Plate
  - **Internal Padding:** Silent Foam + Tuning Paper + Acoustic Film + PU Foam + Silicone Base Pad
  - **Keycaps:** ABS Side-print
- **Lighting & Software:**
  - **Backlight:** ARGB
  - **Driver:** Web Driver & HUB Support
  - **Functions:** **SOCD**, TGL, MT, Macro, MPT, END
- **Colors:** Black / White

## 🚀 Key Advantages of the ND68 Series

- **Advanced Algorithm:** The 2nd Generation G Turbo algorithm ensures that magnetic switch performance remains stable even under temperature fluctuations.
- **Ultra-Low Latency:** With the Pro version achieving 0.08ms, it is optimized for high-intensity FPS titles such as *VALORANT*, *CS2*, and *Apex Legends*.
- **SOCD Support:** Built-in SOCD (Snap Tap equivalent) allows for instantaneous counter-strafing and movement precision.
- **Superior Build:** The combination of an Aluminum plate and a multi-layered Gasket structure provides both durability and a premium acoustic profile.
