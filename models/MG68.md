# IROK MG68 Full Aluminum Magnetic Keyboard Series

The MG68 series is a premium 65% (68-key) lineup featuring CNC Full Aluminum construction and high-performance magnetic switch technology.

---

## 1. MG68 Ace (Performance Wired)
The standard-setter for aluminum magnetic keyboards, focusing on pure wired performance.

### 🛠 Technical Specifications
- **Connection:** Wired (High-speed USB-C)
- **Polling Rate:** 8000Hz (8K)
- **Latency:** 0.1ms
- **Accuracy:** 0.005mm
- **Scanning Rate:** 128k Single-key / 16k Full-key
- **Switch:** Purple Star Magnetic Switch
- **Plate:** Metal Positioning Plate
- **Internal Padding:** PO Plate Foam + PO Case Foam
- **Keycaps:** ABS Side-print
- **Case Finish:** Anodized (Black/Red) or Electrophoresis (White)

---

## 2. MG68 Ace W (High-End Tri-Mode)
The ultimate wireless magnetic keyboard with a massive battery and dual-8K performance.

### 🛠 Technical Specifications
- **Connection:** **Tri-mode (Wired / 2.4G Wireless / Bluetooth)**
- **Battery:** **10000mAh Ultra-large Capacity**
- **Polling Rate:** **Wired 8000Hz / 2.4G 8000Hz**
- **Latency:** 0.1ms
- **Accuracy:** 0.005mm
- **Scanning Rate:** 256k Single-key / 32k Full-key
- **Switch:** Purple Star Magnetic Switch
- **Plate:** Metal Positioning Plate
- **Internal Padding:** PO Plate Foam + PO Case Foam
- **Keycaps:** ABS Side-print
- **Case Finish:** Anodized (Black/Red) or Electrophoresis (White)

---

## 3. MG68 Plus (Flagship Wired)
The flagship model with the highest accuracy, lowest latency, and premium material build.

### 🛠 Technical Specifications
- **Connection:** Wired (High-speed USB-C)
- **Accuracy:** **0.001mm (Ultra-precision)**
- **Latency:** **0.08ms (Esports Grade)**
- **Polling Rate:** 8000Hz (8K)
- **Scanning Rate:** 256K Hz
- **Switch:** Purple Star / Star Light Magnetic Switch
- **Plate:** **FR4 Gold-plated Positioning Plate**
- **Internal Padding:** Poron Plate Foam + PCB Film + EPDM Switch Pad + Silicone Bottom Pad
- **Keycaps:** **PC Frosted Keycaps**
- **Protection:** **PCB Nano Waterproofing**
- **Case Finish:** Anodized (Black/Red) or Electrophoresis (White/Pink)
- **Special Features:** Ribbon/Tag Design + Music Rhythm support

---

## 🚀 Shared Core Features (All MG68 Models)

- **Build:** CNC Full Aluminum Alloy Case
- **Structure:** Esports-grade Ultra-stable Gasket Mount
- **Algorithm:** **Gturbo Magnetic Algorithm** (Auto-calibration & Temperature Drift Resistant)
- **Lighting:** ARGB (Full-range Spacebar with 3 dedicated LEDs)
- **Driver:** IROK Self-developed Web Driver & Hub Support
- **Key Functions:** KME, DKS, MT, TGL, Full Key Macro
- **Compatibility:** Windows
